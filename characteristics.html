Characteristics

Detailed characteristics of GM Motors' cars

Chevrolet Spark

Chevrolet Spark is the result of the work of leading designers from different countries. The Spark, with its impressive two-tier radiator grill and sharp contours, will surely attract attention wherever it is. In addition, due to its compact size and maneuverability, Spark is able to "integrate" into any urban movement.

Spark's cabin is very spacious, which can accommodate a load of 170 liters, and when folding the rear seats in a ratio of 60:40, this space increases to 568 liters. Outside, the Chevrolet Spark looks very compact, but 5 people can easily accommodate in its spacious interior.

In the city it’s a compact and maneuverable car, on the highway it sensitively responds to all the driver’s commands, and most importantly - wherever you are, it’s just nice to drive. In short, the Chevrolet Spark is a new step in the automotive industry.

You did not expect this - the dashboard is in a motorcycle style! Moreover, the dashboard is positioned so that it can be easily captured by the eye, while it is very compact and easy to use. The analogue speedometer of the new Spark is complemented by a digital tachometer, which is highlighted in blue when the side lights are turned on. In addition to aesthetic benefits, expressive design should also bring practical benefits. Highly located taillights that not only look great, but also contribute to safety in low visibility conditions. The raised wheel arches help improve aerodynamics and reduce fuel consumption.

Chevrolet Gentra

Chevrolet Gentra - this is the car whose appearance will not leave indifferent any car enthusiast. The right proportions, streamlined shapes, embossed lines harmoniously combine in this car, which has a respectable interior and exterior. Gentra comfortably accommodates 5 people in a modern, sleek interior. The dashboard has a sense of proportion and a variety of options. The arrangement of the chrome-plated devices is carefully thought out. Its driving performance has been thoroughly tested and perfected.

The streamlined, modern body design of the Chevrolet Gentra is a merit of the famous Italian Pininfarina car studio. The 4-door Gentra with an elegant design and a spacious interior is not just a family sedan, it is a clear confirmation that a practical car does not require compromises. This is the embodiment of quality from and to, with a lot of additional features and excellent handling.

The appearance of the car was created by Italian experts, so it looks pretty impressive. The main feature of the model is smooth, rounded lines, thanks to which it seems much larger and more voluminous than it actually is. At the same time, the appearance and dynamics are noble inherent in cars of a more expensive class. All exterior details, starting with 15-inch wheels and ending with the original side glazing, are distinguished by quality and Italian sophistication.

Chevrolet Captiva 5

An engine with a volume of 3 liters develops a power of 258 horsepower. A great choice for those who value the versatility and practicality of a comfortable and safe SUV.

Maximum equipment with a high-quality interior will not leave indifferent even the most demanding SUV fans.

The Chevrolet Captiva crossover is designed specifically for the modern family.

Captiva offers you a lot of bright features such as reliability, durability and spaciousness of a real SUV.

Chevrolet Captiva is very roomy - you can not say otherwise. When the rear seats are folded, the car’s impressive interior space reaches an impressive volume of 1,577 liters. The driver's seat with lumbar support and electric drive can be adjusted in eight directions. Add to this a spacious third row and many customization options that will allow you to place any luggage. You can choose a 5- or 7-seater version to accommodate all passengers. Chevrolet Captiva is also available with a skylight. All this will allow you to share any adventures with those whom you take with you.

Chevrolet Malibu 2

In terms of strength, the Chevrolet Malibu body is not inferior to other representatives of the mid-size sedan segment in the world market, due to which precise handling and control over the road are achieved. In addition, the steering gear of the premium level provides a smooth ride, clear feedback and traction.

Salon Chevrolet Malibu - one of the most spacious and comfortable in its class. Thanks to the large width of the body, Malibu offers more space for passengers and luggage. The design of the car uses special sound-absorbing materials; McPherson front struts and multi-link independent rear suspension, and the engine is located in a special insulated block, which prevents noise from entering the cabin. This is embodied in the silhouette of the car, resembling a coupe, and in every detail. You no longer have to spend time looking for a key in a bag or briefcase - since you can remotely open the car's doors and start the engine, just by pressing a button on the toolbar. This is standard equipment for the LTZ. The rigid Malibu body structure not only contributes to better handling, but also protects car passengers. Wide, comfortable seats and a spacious lounge with many storage compartments - all this creates a feeling of well-being. Sound-absorbing materials used in the design of the car, independent suspension and an insulated engine subframe make the interior of the new Malibu cozy and quiet.

Chevrolet Tracker

Chevrolet Tracker 2018 is an updated (restyled) crossover model, which is part of the K1 category car group. Compactness and practicality are the qualities that distinguish this car from others.

The Chevrolet Tracker includes all the qualities inherent in new crossovers - this is railing, body trim, increased clearance and an extended track. The appearance of the car, compared to previous models, looks more taut and looks harmonious, the front of the new crossover looks very aggressive, due to optics.

The front of the Chevrolet Trax is equipped with LED daytime running lights, a beautiful embossed bumper, a standard protective sponge, and an updated radiator grille, which consists of two grilles. The protective lip reduces the distance between the car and the track.

The tracker also warns of leaving a busy lane and is equipped with a towing system and dynamic stabilization of the car on the roads. A set of systems improves the comfort, stability and reliability of the crossover on the highway and off-road.

Chevrolet Orlando

Chevrolet Orlando is a five-door seven-seat family minivan (MPV). The car is intended for family use and a corporate fleet of cars. Chevrolet Orlando is equipped with modern passive and active safety systems.

The car is equipped with a four-channel anti-lock braking system (ABS), brake force distribution (EBD), traction control (TCS), electronic stability control (ESP) and emergency braking assistance (BreakAssist). All versions of the Chevrolet Orlando are equipped with front and side airbags, as well as curtains for the first and second row.
