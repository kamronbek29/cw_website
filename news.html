GM Uzbekistan plans to launch new cars in the budget segment

GM Uzbekistan plans to launch production of new cars in the budget segment in the coming years, said Rustam Kodirov, head of the strategic planning, analytics and development department of Uzavtosanoat. ...

“We have plans to produce budget cars. So far, unfortunately, I cannot disclose this, because there are processes, there is an agreement on non-disclosure of information, ”Kodirov said.

According to him, in the next five years, the company expects that the main demand will fall on the budget segment of the car market.

“I can say with confidence that it will be completely new models and their production will begin in parallel with the whole world,” the company representative added.

He also said that the Asaka plant could begin assembling Chevrolet Trailblazer, Chevrolet Equinox and Chevrolet Traverse cars by the end of 2019.

“If I'm not mistaken, somewhere in December, or at the beginning of next year, they will start [to produce]. Their volume will be very small. It is necessary to focus primarily on demand. It makes no sense to produce hundreds of thousands of Trailblazer that they won’t buy, ”Kodirov told reporters.

GM Uzbekistan announces the start of production of three new models

GM Uzbekistan may launch production of Chevrolet Trailblazer, Chevrolet Equinox and Chevrolet Traverse cars in December 2019, Rustam Kodirov, head of the strategic planning, analytics and development department of Uzavtosanoat, said. ...

“If I'm not mistaken, somewhere in December, or at the beginning of next year, they will start [to produce]. Their volume will be very small. It is necessary to focus primarily on demand. It makes no sense to produce hundreds of thousands of Trailblazer that they won’t buy, ”Kodirov told reporters.

Recall that the new SUV models were presented to President Shavkat Mirziyoyev on May 17 in Andijan. GM Uzbekistan plans to bring the localization level of new models to 50%.

Earlier, the deputy board of Uzavtosanoat Aziz Shukurov announced the production of three new models on a global chassis since the beginning of 2021, as well as the organization of the coarse assembly of 2 types of SUV (Sport Utility Vehicle) class cars from the third quarter of 2019.

GM Uzbekistan offers Tracker at a discount of 16 million in interest-free installments for 2 years

GM Uzbekistan congratulates everyone on Ramadan and offers Tracker Premier AT with a 2-year interest-free installment plan with a discount of 16 million soums.

To do this, the buyer must pay an initial payment of 128 million soums, and the remaining amount must be paid within 2 years. ...

In addition, taking into account the numerous offers received from buyers, the company also increased the number of cars that can be purchased in interest-free installments.

Now the Chevrolet Lacetti 3-position car can also be purchased in interest-free installments for 1 year.

For this, the buyer needs to pay 100 million soums and pay the remaining amount of 28 million soums without interest for 1 year.

It should be noted that a buyer who buys a car in installments for 1 year pays out an insurance policy against financial risks in the amount of 2%; also a pledge insurance policy in the amount of 1% and for 2 years 3% of financial risks and a 2% pledge insurance policy.

These insurance payments are used to cover existing debts and accidents.

Buying on favorable conditions Lacetti will be available until June 10, and Tracker Premier AT - until June 12.
